A Modern Dental Clinic Built for You

Tooth Planet Dental Care Clinic & Implant Centre is a state-of-the-art dental facility located in the heart of Kharar, opposite Civil Hospital. We bring the finest in dental technology and personalised care under one roof.

From routine check-ups and cleanings to advanced surgical procedures, laser dentistry, and full-mouth restoration — our expert team handles it all with precision, empathy, and the highest standards of hygiene.
